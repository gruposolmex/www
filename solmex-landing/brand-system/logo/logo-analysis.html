01 — Anatomy

Logo Construction

The Solmex mark is composed of two interlocking "S" forms representing the seamless flow of intelligent logistics networks.

  • 01
    Upper Flow Element
    The upper "S" curve represents outbound logistics flow—goods moving from origin to network. Features a dynamic trailing edge suggesting speed and efficiency.
  • 02
    Lower Flow Element
    The mirrored lower form represents inbound logistics—completing the cycle. Together they create an infinite loop of operational continuity.
  • 03
    Central Nexus
    The white circular core represents the command center—the intelligent hub where all logistics data converges. It symbolizes clarity, precision, and real-time visibility.
  • 04
    Gradient Energy
    The vertical gradient (Orange → Gold) represents the sunrise—a new era of logistics. It flows from energy (#FF9C37) to optimism (#FFD81C).
