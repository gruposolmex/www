02 ‚Äî Visual Style

Aesthetic Guidelines

The visual qualities that define Solmex photography.

Lighting
  • Golden hour and blue hour preferred
  • Strong directional light for drama
  • Industrial artificial lighting at night
  • High contrast with deep shadows
  • Backlit silhouettes for scale
Mood & Tone
  • Confident and professional
  • Industrial yet human
  • Scale and grandeur
  • Precision and order
  • Forward-looking optimism
Textures
  • Weathered steel and metal
  • Concrete and asphalt
  • Rust and patina (controlled)
  • Clean modern surfaces
  • Industrial patterns and repetition
Focus & Depth
  • Sharp focus on key subjects
  • Shallow depth for portraits
  • Deep focus for wide operations
  • Motion blur for movement
  • Crisp detail on equipment

05 ‚Äî Guidelines

Do's & Don'ts

Rules for maintaining photographic brand integrity.

‚úì Do
  • Show scale with human elements for reference
  • Capture golden hour light when possible
  • Include brand colors naturally in environment
  • Show clean, organized operations
  • Feature diverse workforce authentically
  • Use high-resolution, sharp imagery
  • Show technology and modern equipment
  • Capture motion and activity
‚úï Don't
  • Use stock photos that feel generic
  • Over-saturate or heavily filter images
  • Show messy or disorganized scenes
  • Include visible safety violations
  • Use dated or obsolete equipment
  • Apply trendy filters or effects
  • Crop logos or branding awkwardly
  • Use low-resolution or blurry images
